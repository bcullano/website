Virtual Concert

NJIT String Ensemble

Click on the image on the right to see the video!

Due to the COVID pandemic, the NJIT string ensemble did not meet in person and came up with the idea to create a virtual concert. To do this, each student would record their part at the decided tempo and upload it. Then, the conductor, Christine Sweet, gather all of the recordings and the video is produced by her husband, Brennan Sweet. This began in the Spring 2020 semester when the ensemble performed Don't Stop Believin' which was very successful. This virtual concert project continues in the 2020-2021 school year. I did not participate in the Don't Stop Believin' project due to conflicting schedule at the time.
