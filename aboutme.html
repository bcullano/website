About Me

Who I am

I am a 21 year old Mechanical Engineering student in his fourth year at the New Jersey Insitute of Technology. I live in Bridgewater, New Jersey and am glad to go to school close to home so that I can still be with my family.

Engineering

As a kid, I knew I wanted to go into STEM but for most of my life, I wasn't sure what I wanted to study. Towards the end of my high school career, I decided to go into engineering because I wanted to use my skills in math and science to create and solve. I declared a mechanical engineering at the end of my first year in college and ever since, I have been happy with my decision.

Interests

My two main interests/hobbies are music and gaming. Like any other nerdy kid, I grew up interested in video games and the versatility that they offer. They can be simple and goofy, they can be complex and tell timeless stories, or they can even have a mix of both. As for music, I've been a violinist since I was 10 years old, and in recent years, I have been learing piano and guitar on my own. On the side are some of my favorites.
